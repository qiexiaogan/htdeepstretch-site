HT DeepStretch Studio is an Oakland-based one-on-one assisted stretch studio offering more than just passive flexibility work.

We guide our clients through personalized, interactive stretching sessions that blend assisted movement with mindful participation. Our goal is to empower individuals to understand their own bodies, build sustainable flexibility habits, and embrace a healthier, more active lifestyle.

We focus on education and long-term wellness—not just temporary relief.

Classic Stretch

Overview: A full-body assisted stretch designed to deliver consistent, effective stretches that enhance flexibility, improve range of motion, and leave you feeling refreshed and revitalized.

Best For: Clients seeking a balanced, restorative stretch to improve mobility, maintain flexibility, and support overall physical performance — ideal for regular wellness maintenance and active lifestyles.

Deep Stretch

Overview: A highly personalized, deeper-intensity stretching session that pinpoints tight muscle groups, releases deeper adhesions in the fascia and soft tissues, and aligns the body — leaving you feeling re-charged and re-energized.

Best For: Clients who enjoy or can tolerate a more intensive stretch, including athletes, active individuals, and those seeking to boost performance, target chronic tightness, and speed up recovery.
